ABOUT

I’m an aspiring web designer and front-end developer passionate about crafting modern, user-friendly digital experiences. With a background in computer engineering, a keen eye for aesthetics and a deep understanding of code, I always wanted to blend creative design with functional development to bring websites to life.
My approach is based on clean design, modern minimalism, strong visual hierarchy, and smooth interactions. Whether it’s designing sleek interfaces, optimizing user experiences, or coding custom web solutions, I strive to make every project both good looking and intuitive.
Currently, I’m sharpening my skills in Vite, React, and Tailwind CSS while building custom WordPress themes and static websites. I believe that great design isn’t just about how something looks but how it works, ensuring every website is as enjoyable to use as it is to see!
