about

Hi! I'm the face behind ENJOY Web Studio, an aspiring web designer and front-end developer based in Greece. This little studio is a project born out of a love for building clean, modern, and user-friendly digital experiences.

My journey into web design wasn’t exactly a straight line. With a background in electrical & computer engineering and a creative streak that never quite fit into engineering school, I spent years bouncing between the technical and the artistic. Eventually, I realized I didn’t have to choose, and that's when I began pursuing web design as a career.

Now, I like to spend my time designing and developing websites that not only look good but feel good to use. I’m especially drawn to minimal aesthetics, intuitive design, and code that works quietly in the background to support the user experience.

When I’m not behind a screen, you’ll probably find me sketching ideas, tinkering with something new, or just looking for inspiration online.

Let’s build something great together!
